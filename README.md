## Project Description
This project analyzes the thermal behavior of lithium-ion batteries used in drone systems under heat stress conditions.

The objective of this work is to evaluate temperature rise, heat generation, and thermal runaway behavior in drone battery modules. Simulation-based models were used to analyze battery thermal performance and identify safety limits.

## Simulation Analysis
The study includes the following analyses:

- Temperature rise during abnormal operation
- Heat generation vs heat dissipation
- Battery safety temperature limits
- Thermal runaway propagation between cells
- Drone battery voltage profile
- 3D temperature distribution in battery pack

## Key Outcome
The results demonstrate that excessive current loads and insufficient heat dissipation can lead to thermal runaway in lithium-ion batteries used in drone systems.

Proper thermal management and battery monitoring systems are essential to improve drone safety and reliability.
